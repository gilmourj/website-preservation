Distribution Warehouse

  • English

You can order publications from the FEMA Distribution Center. Download the order form and provide:

  • The resource's title
  • FEMA publication ID number
  • Quantity of each publication requesting
  • Your name, address, zip code and daytime phone number

Note: Some resources might have restrictions on the quantity allowed per order, per customer. Also, please review if the resource is currently back-ordered.

You can order hard copies by emailing, calling or faxing the FEMA Distribution Center using the order form. 

Work hours for phone orders are Monday - Friday, 8 a.m. - 5 p.m., Eastern Standard Time.
