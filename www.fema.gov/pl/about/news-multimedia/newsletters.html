Newsletters

  • English

FEMA has several publicly available newsletters that we email to subscribers. The newsletters range from information for emergency managers to location or region specific material for the general public in that area.

Subscribe

When you subscribe to a newsletter, we ask for your email address and then you select all the newsletters you would like to subscribe to from a list. Your email address will only be used to deliver the information you've requested and allows you to access your GovDelivery account. Learn more in our Privacy Policy.

You can change or cancel your subscriptions at any time.

FEMA Bulletin

The FEMA Bulletin highlights the agency's recent and upcoming program and policy activities and announcements. You can subscribe to receive the FEMA Bulletin directly on Tuesday afternoons.
