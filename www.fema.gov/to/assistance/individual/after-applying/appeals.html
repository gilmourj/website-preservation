ʻOku ʻikai fakahā atu ʻi he peesi ko ʻeni ʻa e fakaikiiki fakamuimuitaha ʻo e tokoni fakafoʻituitui ki he ngaahi fakatamaki naʻe fakaha ʻi he ʻaho 22 ʻo Maʻasi 2024.

Toe fekumi ange ki he ngaahi me'a 'oku ma'u 'i he taimi ni.

Kapau te ke maʻu ha tohi ʻo fakahaaʻi ai ʻoku íkai ke ke kau í he maʻu tokoni pe ʻoku ʻikai kakato hoʻo tohi kole, te ke kei lava pe ʻo fakakakato ʻa e tohi kole pe fakahoko haó tangi fekauáki mo e tuʻutuʻuni ʻi loto ʻi he ʻaho ʻe 60 ʻo hono maʻu á hoó tohi tuʻutuʻuni. ʻE meili atu ʻa e tohi kiate koe pe fakahu ki hoʻo ákauni í he Senita Tokoni ki he Fakatamaki, kapau kuo ke fokotuʻu ha ʻakauni.

Ko e tohi tangi ko ha tohi kole ki he FEMA ke toe vakaiʻi hoʻo faile, pea mo ha faingamalie ke ʻomi ha fakamatala foʻou pe lahi ange naʻe ʻikai fakahu kimuʻa ʻe ala uesia ai ʻa e tuʻutuʻuni. Te ke lava ʻo fai ha tangi ki ha faʻahinga tuʻutuʻuni á e FEMA fekauʻaki mo hoʻo tohi kole ki he Tokoni Fakafoʻituitui, hange ko hoʻo ʻuluaki fili ke ke kau, lahi pe faʻahinga tokoni ʻoku ʻoatu kiate koe, ngaahi tohi kole tomui, ngaahi kole ke fakafoki ha paʻanga, pe ko hano fakaʻikaiʻi ʻo e Tokoni Fakataimi ki he Nofoʻanga Fakataimi.

Kapau ʻoku ʻi ai haʻo ngaahi fehuʻi fekauʻaki mo e tohi naʻa ke maʻu pe founga ke faile ai ha tangi, te ke lava ʻo fetuʻutaki ki ha Laine tokoni á e Fakafofonga ó e FEMA ki he 1-800-621-3362. Te ke lava foki ʻo fili ke kole ha tatau ʻo hoʻo faile mei he FEMA ke tokoni ke mahino kiate koe ʻa e ʻuhinga ó e tuútuúni ʻoku ke fie tohi tangi ai. Te ke lava foki ʻo fili ke kole ha tatau ʻo hoʻo faile mei he FEMA ke tokoni ke mahino kiate koe ke ke mahinoí ʻa e ʻuhinga naʻa ke maʻu ai ʻa e tuútuúni ʻoku ke fie tohi tangi ki ai.

É Anga Fefe haó Tohi Tangi Ki Ha Tuútuúni?

Fakamatalaʻi ʻi hoʻo tohi ʻa e ʻuhinga ʻoku ke pehe ai ʻoku hala ʻa e tuútuúni fekauʻaki mo e lahi pe faʻahinga tokoni naʻa ke maʻu. Kuopau ke ke fakamoʻoni hingoa ʻi he tohí, pe ko ho kaungā-kolé.

Kapau te ke fili ke ʻi ai ha hoa ngaue hono tolu ʻoku ne fakahu mai ha kole maʻau, kuopau ke fakamoʻoni hingoa ʻa e tohi tangi ʻe he hoa ngaue hono tolu. ʻIkai ngata ai, fakakau ai ha fakamatala naʻa ke fakamoʻoni hingoa ki ai, ʻo fakamafaiʻi ʻa e hoa ngaue hono tolu ke ne fai maʻau á e tohi tangi ki he tuʻutuʻuni, tukukehe kapau kuo ʻosi ʻi he faile ʻa e ngaahi fakamatala fakapepa ko ia.

Ka fiemaú ha tatau ʻo hoʻo faile, kuopau ke ke fakahu ha tohi kole ʻoku kau ai ho hingoa kakato, fika ʻo e tohi kole FEMA, fika ʻo e fakatamaki, tuʻasila ʻo e koloa ne maumau mo ho tuʻasila meili lolotonga, ho ʻaho faʻeleʻi mo hoʻo fakamoʻoni hingoa mo e taha ʻo e ngaahi meʻa ni:

  • Ko ha sitapa pe sila, pe
  • Ko ha fakamatala ʻoku pehe, "ʻOku ou fakaha heni fakatatau ki he tautea ʻo e fuakava loi ʻoku moʻoni mo totonu ʻa e fakamatala ni."

Fakakau ai ha faáhinga fakamatala fakapepa felaveí, hangē ko hono fakafuofuaʻi ʻe he tokotaha ngāue aleapaú pe ngaahi tohi fakaíkaií mei he ngaahi kautaha maluʻí ʻaki hoʻo tohi tangi kole.

É Anga Fefe haó Tohi Tangi?

Te ke lava ʻo fakahu hoʻo tohi tangi mo e fakamatala fakapepa ʻi he ʻinitaneti, é ha tokotaha,ʻi he meili pe makoni.

ʻI he ʻInitaneti

ʻE lava ke tokangaʻi ʻa e ngaahi tohi tangi ʻi he ʻinitaneti. ʻAʻahi ki he DisasterAssistance.gov ke faʻu ha ʻakauni pea fakahu kotoa ai e ngaahi fakamatala fakapepa ʻo fakaʻaongaʻi ʻa e "Senita Fakahu"    

ʻE ha tokotaha

Te ke lava ʻo ʻave hoʻo tohi tangi ki ha Senita Fakaakeake mei he Fakatamaki.

ʻI he Meili

Meili mai hoʻo tohi tangi mo e ngaahi fakamatala fakapepa felaveí ki he:

FEMA - Individuals & Households Program National Processing Service Center
(FEMA – Senita Tokoni Fakafonua ki he Polokalama Fakafo’ituitui & Ngaahi Famili)
P.O. Box 10055
Hyattsville, MD 20782-8055

Í he Makoni

Makoni hoʻo tohi tangi mo e ngaahi fakamatala fakapepa felaveí ki he:

(800) 827-8112
Attention: FEMA - Individuals & Households Program
(Ki he: FEMA – Polokalama Fakafo’ituitui & Ngaahi Famili)

Ke toe fakakaukauí, kuopau ke meili hoʻo tohi tangi ʻi loto ʻi he ʻaho ʻe 60 mei he ʻaho ʻo e tohi tuʻutuʻuni.

Hili hono Fakahu hoʻo Tohi Tangi

ʻOku toe vakaiʻi ʻa e ngaahi tohi tangi kotoa pe. Óku fakahoko á e ngaahi tuútuúnin í loto í he áho é 30 mei hono maú ó e tohi tangi, kaekehe, ʻe aʻu nai ia ki he ʻ aho ʻ e 90 ki ha tuʻutuʻuni.

ʻE lava ke kole atu ha fakamatala lahi ange meiate koe kapau ʻoku ʻikai maʻu ʻe he FEMA ha fakamatala feʻunga ke fai ha tuútuúni.

ʻE fakaha atu kiate koe ʻi he tohi ʻa e tali ki hoʻo tohi tangi, ʻi he meili pe ʻi he ʻakauni DisasterAssistance.gov naʻa ke faʻu ʻi he taimi naʻa ke kole ai ki he FEMA.

Tohi Tangi ki he Tanaki ʻo e moʻua ʻo e niʻihi fakafoʻituitui mo e ngaahi famili (moúa teéki totongi)

Kapau naʻa ke maʻu ha Tohi Fanongonongo Ngalingali é Moúa, ʻoku mahuʻinga ke lau fakalelei ʻa e tohi. Te ke lava ʻo fakahu mai ha tohi tangi, ʻa ia te ke lava ʻo fili ai ke kole faingamalie kenau fanongo ki haó fakaha hoó keisi, ʻi loto ʻi he ʻaho ʻe 60 mei hono maʻu ʻo e tohi. Ko e faingamalie fanongo ni, ke ʻoatu ai ha fakamoʻoni mahuʻinga (e.g., fakamoʻoni pe ngaahi fakamatala fakapepa kehe) ke poupouʻi hoʻo keisi.

ʻOku seti ʻa e ngaahi taimi ki he fanongoa á e ngaahi fakaha keisi ʻi he taimi ʻoku fakapapauʻi ai ʻe he FEMA he ʻikai lava ke fakaleleiʻi ʻataʻata pe ʻa e fehuʻi ʻo e moʻua ʻaki hano toe vakaiʻi ʻo e faile pepa. ʻAʻahi ki he peesi ki he Ngaahi Tangi Óku Fanongoa Fekauáki Pea mo e Tanaki Moúa ʻa e Niʻihi Fakafoʻituitui mo e Ngaahi Famili (Moúa Teéki Totongi) ke ako lahi ange ki he founga ngaue.
