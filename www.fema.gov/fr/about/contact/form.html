Contact

If you need help after a disaster, go to DisasterAssistance.gov.

If you need specific information about your case, please email us at askIA@fema.dhs.gov, call the FEMA Helpline at 1-800-621-3362, or visit a Disaster Recovery Center (DRC Locator).

Please avoid including sensitive information, such as your social security number, banking information, home or mailing address, or other personal information in this form. 

Privacy Information

FEMA collects and uses personal information to help people who apply for disaster assistance. We do this under the authority of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act and the privacy rules listed in the Disaster Recovery Assistance Files notice.

We might keep emails for internal quality checks or to update your records, if necessary. For more on how we handle your information, see the FEMA Privacy Policy.
