Tumunu Pisekin Eom Family Kena Mi Auchea

Quick Tips

Taropwen Pungun Poraus kena

Apachenongen Nenien Angei Aninis

FEMA me pwan ewe Smithsonian Institution ra kan co-sponsoreni ewe Heritage Emergency National Task Force, ew angangfengen ren nap seni 60 mwichen aninis non unusen fonu kena me pwan ofesin federal kena ra forita ren ar repwe tumunu pisekin eorienien armas kena seni ewe foforun katano seni katano kena seni nature me pwan ekkoch atepwanepwan kena.

Ewe Task Force a kan awora ewe amwen mi tapweto ren aninisum ren eom kopwe angeisefani an eom family kewe pisek mi auchea seni ew katano.

Teten kena ngeni Tumunu Pisekin Eom Family Kena Mi Auchea

Fiti ew kukunun mesonoson me pwan mutirin fofor, tumunun sasing kena mi auchea, taropwe kena, paintings kena me pwan ekkoch mettoch kena rese tongeni siwin a kan tufich. Ekkei afanefanen teten seni teten repwene anisuk ne foforieochunano eom kewe mettoch mi auchea me pwan awora ngonuk fansoun eom kopwe fori ew finita seni kaeo won napenon tumunun me pwan kawoten ren an eomuwe family kewe pisekin achechemeni kena.

  1. Akaewenano: En kosapw tongeni eom kopwene tumunu meinisin, iwe awena eom ekkiek won met kena ra kan fakkun auchea ngonuk, ika ren wuruwon, ren moni, ika achechem popun kena.
  2. Forata Neni: Wonetawow mettoch kena ren ar repwe apwaseno ra kan amasowawow napenon neni! Kopwene kan ekkiekieochu foforun kena: apwetetei eom apechata sasing kena ngeni seni apwasen mengaku kena, forata ew hammock ren apwas seni plastic skrinin nukuwow a kan teipino nefinen ruwow sea kena, apungano ew pwangen nefiner non ew rack ren apwas fiti tulle ika tukumi ekkewe mecha fiti insulation ren paip ren napenon aninisin.
  3. Fofor ngeni fiti Tumun: Eaea napenon tumun non amwokutun eom pisek auchea kena, ina a kan tongeni akaewin mecheres ne tano nupwen a chochon.

Quick Tips

Pweteten apwasen seni asepwan a kan eochuno ren meinisin pisekum kena mi auchea -- non imw kena, ika mi tufich. Apwasen chon mekur kena, aiin kena, wuum kena me pwan tamenon pwanon ngeni akar epwene fori katano kena resapw tongeni forsefani. Anepano feinin asepwan non imw fiti saipo kena, suukun asammwacho kena, air conditioners kena, me pwan dehumidifiers kena.

Sasing kena, taropwe kena, buuk kena, me pwan mengaku kena repwe kan afouno ika pwe en kose kan tongeni apwaser me non 48 awa. Tukumi mettoch kena non taropwen afouno me pwan isenirenong non ew frost-free freezer a kan set non ewe tekiakisin tempichu mi tongeni.

Sasing kena

  • Kosapw etifeseni sasing kena mi chochon me pwan negatives kan ina ra kan pachfengen.
  • Ika pwe fansoun a mutngeni, angei echo sasingen noum kewe sasing ren eom kopwe isieni ew digital kapiin ren.
  • Nimeti sasing kena seni eom tonueochur non distilled konik, ika mi tufich, ika non konik mi nimeoch ika pwe en kose kan kuuna distilled konik.
  • Apwasa seni asepwan sasing kena won echo skrin mi plastic ika paper towel, ika seni awuuter seni ew pwokuku fiti plastic clothespins kena.
  • Kosapw mut ngeni ewe sasing an epwe pach ngeni pwan ekkoch neni kena nupwen a kan apwaseno.

Buuk

  • Ika pwe a wor noum buuk kena ra kan nom non konik ra kan monungaw seni chonungawen annow, iwe ir ra kan afeiengaw ngeni pechakunom. Poutireno ika kapas ngeni emon chon angangen tumunun buuk ren afanefanen.
  • Amwokutawow dopwunon sakit kena ren ar repwe eimuno apwasenor.
  • Isenanong paper towels nefinen ekkewe peich ren buuk kena mi chochon.
  • Ren buuk kena ir ra kan ekis chochon ika pekin chok a kan chochon, ika pwe ekkewe buuk ra kan nom non mechor, parang, ika chonun neset, tonuweno ekkewe buuk, ew non ew fansoun, non ew bucket ika tub ren konik mi nimeoch, amwochu ewe buuk non kapunguno me pwan ngutuno nupwen ka uwanong non ewe konik.

Taropwe kena, Kapiin me Sasing kena

  • Amwakutawow paintings kena me pwan sasing kena seni frames kena.
  • Apwasa seni asepwan ika pwe en ka angei ekkoch chok taropwe kena ra chochon ika taropwe kena, are ika en ka angei nafen neni ren eom kopwe apwasa seni asepwan meinisin eom mettoch kena.
  • Apwasa seni choechoenon asepwan echo me echo taropwe kena ika non kukun chuukutan kena ngeni ew quarter inis tekian.
  • Taropwe kena ra kan tongeni afouno ika pwe en ka kan angei fakkun chomong choen kena eom kopwe apwasa. Epwene kan tori ekkoch wik kena tori ekkoch maram kena ren ei foforun.

Taropwen Pungun Poraus kena

Nupwen menau kena ra kan akunutiw seni ew chonuno, hurricane, awuniar ika eef, pisekum kena mi auchea usun chok an family kena pisek mi auchea, sasing kena me pwan ekkoch iseisen pisek mi auchea kena ra kan napeno anomoter. Ewe Heritage Emergency National Task Force a kan awora poputan ekeitin kena seni chon angangen tumunun pisek mi auchea ren ekkena ir ra kan kuut ren -- me pwan kuuna -- pisekin family kena mi auchea nein ekkewe katano.

Apachenongen Nenien Angei Aninis

Seni 1995, ewen Task Force a kan fen awora ngeni emon me emon aramas me pwan ewe nenien nonomun eoreni -- museum kena, library kena, archives kena, mwichen arts kena, rekoto ofes kena me pwan chomong -- fiti ekeitin me pwan aninisin technical me mwan, nupwen me pwan murin katano kena.

Ew napenon ren nenien angei aninis kena a wor won an ewe Task Force we website, mi pachenong ekkewe mi tapweto:

  • Usun ewe Heritage Emergency National Task Force

Ren chomong poraus usun tumunun pisekin eoreni ra afeiengaw ngenir ika ngaweno seni katano kena -- non ekkewe United States me pwan me nukun fonu kena -- churi ewe website ren chienach kena, ewe Smithsonian Cultural Rescue Initiative.
