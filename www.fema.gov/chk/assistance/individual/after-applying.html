Ekei porous eon ei peich ese epuru porous faniten Individual Assistance (Aninis ngeni emon me emon) faniten watten osukokuk e fis non ika mwirin March 22, 2024.

Kaeo met a wor iei.

Uwanong Taropwen

Chechekin Imwom

Ika pwe mi wor noum insurens, iwe kopwe kan fileni ew kleim fiti noumuwe insurens kompeny nupwen en ka aeoeo ren aninisin FEMA. FEMA ese kan tongeni aninis fiti nusuno kena ra kan fen pwonupwon seni insurens. Ika pwe noumuwe insurens ese kan pwonu meinisin eom nusuno kena ika a kan mangeno, iwe meni en kopwene naf ren aninisin FEMA ren eom kewe mochen rese tori.

Ika pwe eomuwe aewin nenien nonom a kan tano seni ewe katano me pwan ese fich nonomun non, nimeoch me pwan tumun, iwe en meni kopwene ateteni ren ew chechekin imwom ren eom kopwe afatano katan. Anongonong won finieom a asisinita non ewe fansoun ren noumuwe aeoeo, en kopwene angei ika echo taropwe ika ponusefan seni electronic. Ewe taropwe epwene aweweni ika en ka naf ren aninis, fita aninis kopwene angei, ifa usun ewe aninis epwene kan nounou, me pwan ifa usun eom kopwe appealini an FEMA we finata ika pwe en kose kan tipeew ngeni.

Eom aninis epwene finita seni awewefengeni eom nusunon auchea kena mi rekotini me pwan watten mochenin kena ngeni ekkewe sakkun aninis ra kawor me non an FEMA program me aninis kena. Aninisin FEMA ese kan wewe ngeni insurens me pwan esapw tongeni fori ewe chon menaweno pwe epwe unuseno. Aninisin federal seni FEMA ra kan chok awora moni kena ren poputan for kena ngeni forun ew imw pwe epwe tumun, nimeoch me pwan tongeni nonom non. En kopwene kan pwan atotono ngeni ewe U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) ren niwinimangen katano kena mi kukun interest rer ren an epwe anepano aninis firi eom pwaksefan.

Chekin Noumuwe Aeoeo won DisasterAssistance.gov

En ka tongeni forata ew online FEMA Disaster Assistance Center (DAC) account non DisasterAssistance.gov. Repwene kan afanefanok pwe kopwe forata ew konon Personal Identification Number (PIN) ren tumunun atotongeni porausen noumuwe aeoeon aninisin katano.

Me non noumuwe online account, en ka tongeni:

  • Cheki eomuwe porausen aeoeon aninisin katano
  • Awora minafon poraus ngeni porausomuwe me pwan mochenum kena
  • Katon taropwe kena me pwan poraus a titingonuk seni FEMA
  • Angei tichikin kena won apachenongen taropwe kena ina FEMA a mochen ren an epwe foforngeni noumuwe aninis
  • Upload taropwe kena ngeni eom file
  • Cheki poraus ina FEMA a kan angei senuk

Afaten Chon Imweimw/Fita Chon non Imw

FEMA epwene kan fori seni annuk ren an epwe afatano an emon chon aeoeo fita chon nom non imwen me pwan afatano imwen io we ika pwe en ka aeoeo ren ekkoch sakkun aninisin imweimw kena.

Katon Taropwen kena ra Apunguno

Afatenon aiititin

Ika pwe FEMA ese kan tongeni an epwe afatano aiititum nupwen fofor ngeni ewe aeoeo, iwe en kopwene fofori eom kopwe uwanong taropwen aninis kena.

Taropwen Aninis ken ren Afaten Aiititum

Taropwen ar repwe afatano aiititum*

  • Taropwen kena seni ewe Social Security Administration, ika pwan ekkoch ofesin federal, a masow ren unusen ika ekkewe nasenon ruwanu maaken ren noumuwe Nampan Social Security (SSN)
  • Katon Social Security ika pwe a titito fiti ew mi katowow seni federal ika state katon aiititum
  • Taropwen niwinum seni nenien eom angang a masow ren unusen ika ekkewe nasenon ruwanu nampa seni noumuwe SSN
  • Katon Aiititum Seni Sonfiu
  • Laisenin pupunu ren an epwe afatano pwaratan ren iten eom family
  • U.S. passport

*Won ew anongonongen case seni case FEMA epwene mutngeni chon aeoeo kena ra nom non U.S territory kena ren ar repwe uwanong fininon taropwen afaten aiititir kena, usun chok katon registerin wutut, me pwan ekkoch.

Ika pwe en ka aeoeo ren aninis fan iten emon kukunun semirit ii emon U.S. citizen ren imwomuwe, iwe en kopwene tinngeni FEMA ekkei mi tapweto:

Echo ekkena taropwe ra maaketiw ngeni ewe peniengaw, ika pwe iten ewe semirit IKA

Noun ewe semirit we taropwen uputiw ME PWAN echo kapiin ren noun ewe semirit we katon Social Security ika taropwen seni ewe Social Security Administration, ika pwan ekkoch ofesin federa, a masow ren unusen ika ekkewe ruwanu nasenon nampa seni noun ewe semirit we SSN.

Uwanongen Taropwe

En ka tongeni churi DisasterAssistance.gov ren eom kopwe uwanong taropwe me pwan cheki ususun noumuwe aeoeo online.

Ra sinei pwe ew online system chok esapw tongeni tori ekkewe mochenin chon menaweno kena, iwe FEMA a pwan forata Documentation Drop-off Centers ikewe chon menaweno kena ra kan tongeni aeoeo ren aninis, eis kapaseis kena, angei nour taropwe kena scannenong non are case file me pwan aniwinisefanir ngenir on-site.

Chechekin Imwom

Murin eom aeoeo ngeni FEMA, eomuwe tungor ren aninis a kan cheki ren ar repwe finata ika ew checheki epwene mochen ren ar repwe afatano pwe kata kena ngeni imwomuwe me pwan pisekin inisum kena ra kan riri ngeni katano we.

An FEMA chechekin imw kena ra kan iei foforino non aramas. An chon menaweno seni katano kewe pechakun me pwan tumun ra kan chuen an FEMA we aewin met repwe fori iwe ewe ofes epwene tumunu an epwe tongeni fori checheki kena seni afateno me nukun nge ika mo pwe ese tonong non ewe imw nupwen chon menaweno kena ra kan noninen pokiten ren met rese sinei usun COVID-19.
