FEMA Grants Outcomes (FEMA GO)

  • English

FEMA Grants Outcomes (FEMA GO) is the grants management system that supports FEMA grants programs. The system allows users to apply, track and manage all disaster and non-disaster grants.

FEMA GO is continually being developed by the Grants Management Modernization (GMM) initiative, which is consolidating existing grant programs into one system.

GRANTS PORTAL

Applicants, recipients and subrecipents login: go.fema.gov

Get answers to frequently asked questions about FEMA GO.

How-To Process Guides

FEMA's grant programs are specific to grants available to states, local, tribal and territorial communities that have experienced a disaster, and grants that are open to all states, local, tribal and territorial governments. The FEMA GO process can vary for each set of grant programs listed below.
